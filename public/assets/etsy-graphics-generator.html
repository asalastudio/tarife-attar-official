FOUR WAYPOINTS TO START WITH

New to Tarife Attar? Not sure where to begin? Here are four waypoints — one from each territory — that we think capture the spirit of the Atlas.

SAANA

Honey Oud • Sana'a, Yemen

The old city climbs. Stone towers rise eight stories into thin mountain air. In Sana'a the beekeepers still scale the high cliffs at dawn, returning with wild honey so dark it tastes of the rock itself. The honey arrives immediately — thick, golden, almost edible — before the oud pulls it downward into something darker and more complex.

Top: Honey, Floral Notes | Heart: Cinnamon, Leather, Jasmine | Base: Vanilla, Oud, Amber

BIG SUR

Coastal Marine • Big Sur, California

The Pacific throws itself against granite and the mist carries everything — wild sage, coastal redwood, the mineral shock of cold saltwater on sun-heated stone. Bergamot and lemon open crisp and maritime. The seaweed accord adds a briny, almost umami depth. The dry-down is ambroxan and cedar: warm, woody, salt-kissed.

Top: Bergamot, Lemon | Heart: Seaweed, Marine Accord | Base: Musk, Ambroxan, Cedar

MEDINA

White Musk • Medina, Saudi Arabia

The Prophet's Mosque glows green at nightfall and the air fills with tahara — the musk of ritual purity. Powdery white musk opens like freshly laundered cotton held to your face — clean, comforting, utterly without pretension. This is the musk that launched a thousand re-orders.

Top: Powdery White Musk | Heart: Soft Florals, Sandalwood | Base: Clean Musk, Creamy Accord

MARRAKESH

Rich Leather • Jemaa el-Fnaa, Morocco

The souk folds around you — leather, cedar, cumin, the metallic ring of hammered brass. Plum and cardamom open rich and heady. The heart is a controlled chaos of rose, cinnamon, nutmeg, and jasmine. The base resolves into leather, oud, and incense. The most complex waypoint on the atlas.

Top: Plum, Cardamom | Heart: Rose, Cinnamon, Jasmine | Base: Leather, Oud, Incense, Cedar
