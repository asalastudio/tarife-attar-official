## DESIGN NOTES FOR CANVA

**Brand Colors:**
- Background: Alabaster #F2F0E9 (warm cream/parchment)
- Text: Charcoal #1A1A1A (near black)
- Accent: Gold #c5a66a (for headers, lines, borders)

**Fonts (or similar in Canva):**
- Headlines: Serif font (like Playfair Display, Cormorant, or Libre Baskerville)
- Body: Same serif, lighter weight
- Tables/Data: Monospace or clean sans-serif

**Style:**
- Clean, editorial, magazine-quality
- Generous white space
- Gold accent lines separating sections
- No flashy graphics — let typography and space speak

**PDF Size:**
- Standard: 8.5" x 11" (Letter) or A4
- Alternative: Square 8" x 8" for modern feel
